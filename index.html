About

Currently, I'm a Research Assistant in the Center for Language and Speech Processing at the Johns Hopkins University, advised by Benjamin Van Durme. Before that, I worked in Beth Irsarel Deaconess Medical Center at Harvard Medical School. My recent research focuses on image recognition, medical data analysis, natural language processing. I am also interested in linguistic semantics.
